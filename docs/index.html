Mobile Bike Computer

Velo is designed for cyclists of all levels whether you are cycling for fitness, training or competitively. The application combines a simple dashboard, an advanced routing and Bluetooth LE hardware sensor integration. All captured data on your ride can be synchronized with your favorite cycling service whether it's Strava for the social community or TrainingPeaks for professional training and coaching.

Dashboard

While You Ride

The dashboard provides a view of your cycling metrics. View data about your speed, cycle time and distance traveled with the ability to synchronize the data to HealthKit or Strava.

Navigation

Point A To B and Back

The dashboard provides a view of your cycling metrics. View your speed, cycle time, and distance traveled with the ability to upload to Strava or store in HealthKit.

Commute

From Point A to B

Commuter routes providing you the option of having the fastest, safest, or least hills.

  • Shortest

    The shortest distance from your location to your destination

  • Least Hills

    The route with the least amount of ascent from your location to your destination

  • Safest

    The route with the most bike lanes and fewest cars from your location to your destination

Loop

Discover New Adventures

Loop routes give you directions to the start of a loop for exploring, exercise, training.

  • Time

    Pick a route based on a length of time that you want to ride. Get automatically re-routed when you’re halfway through your time limit.

  • Distance

    Start cycling in any direction. The automatic re-route engine will route you back to your start location once you’ve ridding long enough to complete your distance.

  • Safest

    Select a route that is known for training specific elements of cycling
